Mach-one PSP Converter v1.03:
-----------------------------

You can use this freeware stuff to convert pictures or other graphical
objects for your PSP codes. 

This version converts
24 bit .bmp -> .c source
24 bit .raw (interlaved RGB) -> .c source

Usage:
m1pspc.exe inputfile outputfile

Known bugs:
none reported
